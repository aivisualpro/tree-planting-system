# Help & Support

## Getting Started
Welcome to the field data collection app. Before heading out, make sure you:
1. Sync all reference data while you have internet.
2. Download the offline maps for your assigned regions.
3. Fully charge your device.

## How to create a visit
1. Tap the "+" button on the visits screen.
2. Fill out the details, add tree species.
3. Ensure you capture a group photo and sign the document.
4. Capture GPS coordinates. If offline, place the pin manually on your downloaded map.
5. Save the draft or submit. Submitted visits will queue for upload.

## What to do when sync fails
If sync fails:
- Check your internet connection.
- Ensure your device has enough storage.
- Go to "Sync Log" to review specific error messages.
- If an image fails to upload, it will automatically retry on the next connection.

## Understanding Sync States
- **Draft**: Saved locally, you can still edit it.
- **Pending**: Queued for upload when internet is available.
- **Synced**: Data has successfully reached the servers.
- **Error**: Failed to sync. Check the sync log.

## Photo & GPS Troubleshooting
- **No GPS Signal**: Step outside to a clear view of the sky. If it still fails, use the offline map to drop a pin manually.
- **Photo won't attach**: Ensure your device storage is not full. The app needs at least 5% free space to function optimally.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I use the app completely offline?**
A: Yes! As long as you have downloaded the reference data and maps beforehand, you can create and save visits entirely offline for days.

**Q: How do I train a new user?**
A: Go to Settings and turn on "Training Mode". This gives you a safe sandbox with fake data where nothing you create will be sent to the real servers.
