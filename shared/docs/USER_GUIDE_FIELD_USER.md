Welcome to the Tree Planting Application. This guide explains how to use the mobile app to record planting visits even when you have no internet connection.

## 1. Getting Started

### Login
1. Open the app and enter your provided email and password.
2. Ensure you have an internet connection **for your first login**.
3. After logging in, the app will download your country and region assignments.

### Offline Mode
- The app is designed to work **without internet**.
- You will see an "Offline" banner when there is no connection.
- You can continue to create and save visits. They will sync automatically when your connection returns.

## 2. Creating a Planting Visit

1. Tap the **"+" (New Visit)** button on the home screen.
2. **Farmer Info**: Select the farmer from the list or add a new one.
3. **Species & Count**: Enter the species planted and the total number.
4. **Photos**: Tap the camera icon to take a photo of the planting site. *Note: Ensure your device's GPS location is enabled.*
5. **Review & Save**: Tap "Save". The visit is now saved securely on your phone.

## 3. Syncing Data

- **Auto-Sync**: The app automatically tries to send saved visits to the server when you connect to Wi-Fi or mobile data.
- **Manual Sync**: Pull down on the home screen list to force a sync attempt.
- **Sync Status**: 
  - 🟢 Green Check: Visit synced successfully.
  - 🟡 Yellow Clock: Visit saved on phone, waiting for internet.
  - 🔴 Red Warning: Sync failed (e.g., photo too large). Tap for details.

## 4. Sending Feedback

If you encounter an issue or bug:
1. Open the side menu and tap **Help & Feedback**.
2. Type a description of the issue.
3. The app will automatically attach your device details and error logs to help us fix it.
4. Tap **Send**.

## 5. Troubleshooting

- **GPS Not Found**: Move outside to a clear area, wait 10 seconds, and try again. Ensure location permissions are granted.
- **Photos Not Uploading**: Ensure you have enough storage space on your phone and try connecting to a stronger network.
- **App Crashing**: Force close the app and reopen. Your saved visits are secure and will not be lost.
